2. Offerings Overview:
   - Distribution / Retailer Finance
   - Invoice Discounting (ID)
   - Pre-Invoice Discounting (PID)
   - Supplier Invoice Discounting (SID)
   - Term Loans

3. Customer Actions via App/Web:
   - Loan & financing apply
   - Documents upload
   - Loan status track
   - Repayment schedule check
   - Continue incomplete applications

4. Product Details:
   A. Distribution / Retailer Finance
      - Distributors/retailers ko credit provide kiya jata hai
      - Eligibility: Registered distributor/retailer, minimum 1-year business, minimum turnover per policy
      - Documents: Business PAN, GST, bank statements, KYC
      - Queries: Loan limit, repayment mode

   B. Invoice Discounting (ID)
      - Raise invoices and get early payment from Kredmint lenders
      - Documents: Invoice copy, PAN, GST, bank statements, KYC
      - Queries: Fund transfer time, multiple invoices

   C. Pre-Invoice Discounting (PID)
      - Working capital based on confirmed purchase orders
      - Documents: PO copy, KYC, GST, bank statements
      - Queries: Difference with ID

   D. Supplier Invoice Discounting (SID)
      - Discount invoices to get early payment
      - Queries: Delay in buyer payment

   E. Term Loans
      - Short-to-mid term loans for business expansion
      - Eligibility: Registered business, 2+ years, positive financials
      - Documents: PAN, GST, bank statements, financial statements
      - Queries: Repayment tenure, prepayment
